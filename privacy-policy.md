Privacy Policy – RealityCheckBot

Last updated: December 2025

RealityCheckBot (“we”, “our”, or “the bot”) is a conversational analysis bot available on platforms such as Telegram and Instagram. Our goal is to provide educational analysis and reality checks on money-making claims shared by users.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to operate the bot:

Message content voluntarily sent by users (text or links)

Platform user ID (used only to send replies)

We do not collect:

Names

Email addresses

Phone numbers

Payment information

Location data

2. How We Use Information

User messages are used solely to:

Analyze money-making claims

Generate an educational “reality check” response

Messages may be temporarily processed by third-party AI services (such as OpenAI) strictly for analysis purposes.

3. Data Storage

We do not permanently store user messages

We do not maintain user profiles

Messages are processed in real-time and discarded

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party APIs to analyze content:

AI language models for educational analysis

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with advertisers or other third parties.

6. User Consent

By sending a message to RealityCheckBot, you consent to:

The processing of your message for analysis

Receiving an automated educational response

7. Educational Disclaimer

RealityCheckBot provides educational information only and does not offer financial, legal, or investment advice. Users are responsible for their own decisions.

8. Children’s Privacy

RealityCheckBot is not intended for users under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
